What’s it called when an employee…

Moves from middle management…

To upper management?

A promotion


Trash jokes aside, I did get a ‘promotion’, and it’s nowhere near management level. I’ve eeked my way out of the barren Service Desk landscape and wandered into the lush garden that is Quality Assurance. That’s right my friends, I now get paid to tell engineers and programmers “no”, and It feels great.

My superiority complex does take a hit when the defect turns out to be my testing methodology and not necessarily their bad coding, but for the time being, I can blame that on “being new”. All in all, it’s still sort of a dream-come-true to be paid to tell people, “sorry, this is unacceptable”.

While the department is nascent, there is a lot of ‘potential’ and other buzzword positivity stuff about our team. I enjoy working with everyone which, in any industry, is the absolute most you can ask for.

There was a pay increase, which was to be expected; but only served to remind me of the brutal income crisis our country is facing. After seeing a 34% pay increase, my high spirits were immediately cratered when I realized my income is essentially table scraps when viewed through a macro lens. I was disgusted with myself for being grateful for a miniscule slice of the Injustice Pie.

After fighting the urge to unionize, I concluded that I’m happy with my new position and consider it a great foundation for my future cybersecurity career.


Happy New Year

Cheers to the future

Wash your hands

Wear a mask
