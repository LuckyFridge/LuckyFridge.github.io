New Contact Option
by Rudy Portillo
8/Mar/2020

A small update to my contact info has come in the inclusion of my PGP Public Key. Clicking on the envelope icon at the top of any page will take you to an email form that will send me an email directly.

Users of this site now have the option of using their own email client to send me an encrypted message using the PGP protocol. This functionality is powered by FlowCrypt.
