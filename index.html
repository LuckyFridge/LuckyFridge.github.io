New Cert
by Rudy Portillo
25/Sep/21

Another day, another cert. Network+ is done and stacked onto my A+. I have now completed the CompTIA™ “trifecta” and am well on my way to being a real Network Boi.

From Day 1, this has been a wild year. From insurgencies, to state-wide freezes, to exploding vehicles (not really, but almost), all the way down to astronomical emergency medical bills; the past nine months have been filled with pedal-to-the-metal shenanigans. Somewhere between all the chaos, I managed to take three cert exams, and pass them all.

Speaking to my successes, I want to credit ‘mental fortitude’ or my ability to ‘steel my resolve’ in the face of Life’s challenges, but the real Secret Sauce is anything but those things. I’ve stumbled and rolled through every metaphorical obstacle course my ambitions subject me to. Every single finish line has seen me heave myself across the threshold, battered, dusty, and unkempt. And as I try to catch my breath, hunched over with hands on my knees, I look up, bleary-eyed at the terrain I’ve covered so far… and I gotta say, I’m pretty proud of myself.
