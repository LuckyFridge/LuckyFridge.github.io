What is this?
by Rudy Portillo
16/Sep/2019
(Click to expand)

 While there is no fully formed and comprehensive plan for this site, it should generally be considered my online resume. It's structured blog-style so that I can keep my ramblings and updates separate from the stuff that's designed to get me a job.

 A cursory inspection will show that this is an simple, static site. I'm currently thinking of how I can spruce up the joint, but I'm not incredibly creative, and I'm terrible at JavaScript, I don't think this thing will ever need a real "back-end" (unless I become popular and need a sign-in form for meet and greets), and I don't even have the kind of time it would take to make this into a modern site.
